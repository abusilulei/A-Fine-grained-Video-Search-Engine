# A-Fine-grained-Video-Search-Engine
Author: Yifei Liu

Date: 2024-06-15

## Overview:

**Combating Clickbait: A Fine-grained Video Search Engine**

"Multimodal Corpora" allows you to search for content in Bilibili (the Chinese counterpart of Youtube) videos based on actual video content rather than titles or descriptions, enabling you to jump directly to the exact moments where your keywords appear while avoiding clickbait.
